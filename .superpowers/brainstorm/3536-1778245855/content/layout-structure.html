Key Design Principles

  • Breathing Space: 80-120px gaps between major sections create visual rhythm and prevent overwhelm
  • Alternating Layouts: Full-width → Constrained → Full-width creates visual variation and interest
  • Typography Hierarchy: Hero dominates, section titles are bold and prominent, body text refined
  • Strategic Accents: Gradients, glows, and colored borders guide focus to key sections
  • Visual Separation: Clear visual breaks between sections with accent dividers and spacing
  • Dynamic Composition: Asymmetrical elements, strategic overflow, unexpected grid breaks
